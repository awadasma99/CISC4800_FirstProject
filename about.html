about me

Hi there! My name is Asma Awad, and I built this site using HTML and CSS. I thought that this would be a great way to combine two interests of mine: coding and baking. When I'm not programming or playing video games, I love to mess around with recipes I find on the internet, putting my own spin on them to make them more of my own.
